OVERVIEW

Our Process

We follow the following steps when developing applications.

  • Design
  • Development
  • Testing


Identifying a common problem is always the creative stage of app development. Once we identify a problem, we design our app with sketches and do surveys to ensure functionality and user experience.

The development phase encompasses the confluence of software development and design. We follow our design guidelines completely before adding additional features.

The software undergoes A/B testing before it is launched and ready to use by the target audience.
